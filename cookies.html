Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugars together until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop spoonfuls onto tray and bake for 10–12 minutes.
