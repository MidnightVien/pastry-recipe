Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a muffin tray with cupcake liners.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, baking soda, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs, oil, and milk in another bowl.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stir until smooth.
  5. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full and bake for 18–20 minutes.
